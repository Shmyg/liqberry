Healthy life

Liqberry is a privately held international company commited to the health and satisfaction
of our customers and highest working standards for our employees.

Liqberry was founded in 2011 as family business and since than has grown to more than 100 employess and several factories across the Europe. Nevertheless, we still believe we're family and strive to make every person in the company happy at the workplace. In return, our employees ensure highest quality of our products and sales process. We never employ 'sell and forget' policy; every customer can always have medical consultation from our doctors team, and we keep history of each customer's requests to be able to better help him. Our goal is to make our customers healthier and help them live better and reacher life. Our slogan is 'Towards the good life!'.
